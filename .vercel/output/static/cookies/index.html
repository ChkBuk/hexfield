Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-24

1. About cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to remember information about you, your preferences and your usage. This policy explains how Hexfield Pty Ltd (ABN 55 698 151 638) uses cookies on this website.

2. Cookies we use

This website is deliberately tracker-free. We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Mixpanel)
  • Advertising cookies
  • Social-media tracking pixels
  • Session-replay tools

The only request your browser makes to a third-party domain is for the Pagefind search assets, which are served from the same origin as this website (no cross-site tracking).

3. Functional storage

The mobile hamburger menu uses a hidden HTML checkbox to track open/closed state. This is stored in-memory by your browser only and is not persisted between page loads or shared with any third party.

4. Form submissions

When you submit the contact form, the data is transmitted to our form-handling infrastructure for processing. Submission data is covered by our Privacy Policy.

5. Your choices

Because we do not set tracking cookies, there is nothing for you to opt out of. You may clear any cookies via your browser settings at any time without affecting site functionality.

Questions? Email info@hexfield.com.au.
